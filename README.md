# digital-gold-token
A professional Web3 staking dashboard for Digital Gold Token ($DGLD), featuring a secure 7-day token locking system, automated yield tracking, real-time gold market charts, and complete crypto wallet connectivity.

📖 How to Stake $DGT
1. Connect Wallet: Click the "Connect Web3 Wallet" button on the dashboard.

2. Enter Amount: Input the amount of $DGT you wish to stake in the "Staking Portal" box.

3. Confirm: Click the "Stake Now (7 Days Lock)" button.

4. Track: You can view your active stake in the "Locked Stake" section, along with your estimated
   rewards. 

5. Claim: After 7 days, click the "Claim Stake & Rewards" button to withdraw your capital and earnings.
